Yamaha WR 155R

Yamaha may soon release its new offroad bike in india names WR 155R as of now it has been launched in indonesia with tthe same name. Unlike the YZF-R15, the MT-15 and the XSR155, the new WR 155R uses a semi-double-cradle frame. In comparison, the road-biased 155 cc duo features a delta-box frame. The off-road-focused styling brings a lean design with minimal body panels to the motorcycle. The front fascia gets a halogen headlight instead of LED units. The headlamp is surrounded by a dual-tone mask. The tall-set front fender enhances the off-road-focused design of the WR 155R.
